    • Welcome to DianAvery

    We appreciate your interest in our British Shorthair Colourpoint Cats and share your enthusiasm for this fascinating breed in this pattern.
    We are a family owned cattery located in NE Lincolnshire, UK. All of our cats are GCCF (Governing Council of Cat Fancy) registered, and we are also members in good standing with TICA (The International Cat Association).
    We are quite busy, but whenever we have the opportunity and time, we exhibit our kittens and cats at shows organised by GCCF or TICA and are proud to have Champions in our breeding cats.
    Our breeding program, which began in 2021, focuses on producing healthy, beautiful and happy British Shorthair Colourpoint kittens with excellent type and nature and with care and love, giving them the best start in life.
    We fully test our BSH Kings and Queens to ensure their health, and we guarantee our kittens are free from genetic diseases.
