    • Our Kittens

    We are extremely proud of our work. We look after our kittens with lots of love and we give them the best possible start in life.
    For the first few weeks of their lives, the kittens stay in our bedroom, making sure they are fine and where mum feels safe. From their birth we are 24/7 around the kittens and mama. We handle all of them as much as possible, so they enjoy human contact. We share photos and videos of the kittens’ progress on our social media, so that the new families feel bonded with their kittens, before our little fluffies rehome in their new lovely homes.
    At 4-5 weeks of age we start to wean our kittens onto a balanced diet and we start litter training them. Most of the kittens are fully litter trained by the age of 6-8 weeks.
    The kittens cannot be formally reserved until they are 6-8 weeks old, and we have made the decision on who would be staying with us.
    Our Kittens are health checked twice and vaccinated by a vet at 9 and 12 weeks old.
    When our kittens are ready for their new home, after at least 13 weeks old, they are fully litter trained, confident, outgoing, healthy and ready to love their new mums and dads.
    To reserve a kitten, we require a non-refundable deposit of £300. We only refund this deposit if we withdraw the kitten from sale.

    Kittens for Sale

    Please note that we do not breed for profit. Our Queens give birth once a year so they have a chance to recover and enjoy the rest of the time.
    The sale of our kittens is performed only from our house.
    We have only purebred BSH from quality producers, they are tested for PKD, ALPS, pd-PRA, FeLV and FIV - CLEAN.
    The photos of our Kings and Queens are presented on the site.
    Every kitten leaves our home with:
    - Certificate
    - Pedigree
    - 5 weeks free insurance
    - Microchipped
    - Fully vaccinated, including Leukemia
    - Flea & worming treatment
    - Kitten pack including food & toys.
