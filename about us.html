    • About Us

    I'm Diana and together with my family, we founded DianAvery - a small hobby cattery with our own registered prefix DianAvery. Since 2021, we've been dedicated to breeding British Shorthair cats, and we're proudly registered with both the GCCF, TICA and some special cat's clubs.
    As a reputable breeder, we strictly follow all guidelines for responsible breeding and uphold a defined code of ethics.

    Our primary goal is to raise healthy and well-socialised British Shorthair kittens that meet the official breed standard. All of our breeding cats have been genetically tested for PKD (Polycystic Kidney Disease), ALPS (Autoimmune Lymphoproliferative Syndrome) and pd-PRA (Progressive Retinal Atrophy) ensuring that our lines are free from any hereditary diseases.
    We have a special love for the Colourpoint coat pattern, which is why our breeding program focuses exclusively on this variety. British Shorthair Colourpoint cats have a lighter body colour with darker points on the ears, face, legs, and tail. They are also known for their calm temperament, sturdy build, and plush, luxurious coat.

    Some of our Queens carry the gene for long hair, so it's possible one day we have British Longhair kittens.

    About Our Kittens

    We are interested in Quality, not Quantity!

    If you are looking for a healthy BSH Colourpoint kitten, then you came to the right place!
    We value the health, well-being, and proper nutrition of our kittens.
    All of our kittens are raised with love and attention in constant contact with us. We raise the kittens as part of our family, so they are very sociable and will easily adapt to their new homes. We let them play freely around the house, with each other or with us. They are perfectly socialised and brought up accustomed to using litter trays and scratching posts.
    All of our kittens do not leave our home until they are at least 13 weeks of age!
